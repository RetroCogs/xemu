Xemu WIP on-line demos

This is a seriously work-in-progress ("WIP") demonstration site of Xemu emulators running inside your web-browser, thanks to the fantastic emscripten toolkit. Please note, that it may not even work for you at all, or you can experience serious bugs. The recommended way is always downloading/installing the native emulator for Windows/Mac/Linux.
